About Us

Prago Infotech is a development hub for planning, building, support and enhancement of web applications.

Company Name : Prago Infotech
Nature Of Business : Web developement
Founded : 2015

Launched back in 2014, Prago Infotech is a world-class full-app-lifecycle software development company, focusing on building web apps, covering all the segments of a successful product launch and maintenance - ideation, prototyping and design, project management, development, QA testing, and support. We work under Agile principles and give each customer’s product the same care and love that we give our own products.

Today we offer application development services to companies that need to scale up their development capabilities while keeping quality, improving productivity, and optimizing costs.
